Iteractions

Iteration 1 | scroll

After the ideas in form, I decided to rethink the form of the physical body. I choose the scroll as the main key in the instellation because it is a physical interface looks like digital device interface. Audience can play with it and easy to dive into my subject with no time to figure out how to use it. And it is also a "scanner" that can show the ownership of the space. I picked different spaces that are symmetric to lead the audience scroll vertically. My wish is to create multiple racks with different locations physical and vertical.
KEY WORDS: ownership | physical space | scroll | perspective





Iteration 2 | moving bar

Other than a scroll, a moving bar is easer for the audience to move and play with. It is also easier for me to install it on the slides.
KEY WORDS: ownership | physical space | drag and pull | perspective


Feedbacks and Reflection

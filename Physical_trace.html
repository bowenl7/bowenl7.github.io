Physical Trace

Inspiration

When I'm thinking about the project, Hito Steyerl's video How Not to be Seen: A Fucking Didactic Educational .MOV File, 2013 gives me a lot of inspirations. This video work is an educational instruction for people to hide in the environment especially from the digital detector-the cameras. People can use physical and technical methods to remove or hide their identity. I like this topic a lot since I was a undergrad. So my questions here are: Can we hide from the digital dector today in the physical world? How do we treat our daily activity data? Maybe some methods in Hito's video can be used.

Physical Trace

The trace in semiotics is a concept developed by Jacques Derrida in Writing and Difference to denote the history that a sign carries with it as the result of its use through time. Words like "black", for example, carry the trace of all their previous uses with them, making them sensitive, loaded words when used in any context. The trace then reveals the possibility for alternative interpretation of concepts, regardless of how carefully articulated they may be, whenever they are expressed in language. Same thing also happens in our physical world. A footprint of human can imply a person's weight, height and dominant hand. A trace of a bear's feces can also indicate its location and health condition. Our personal attributes will be so clear if we open to the world of algorithm, and our trace or signs are our daily activities.

My footprint on the concrete floor will disapper and my trace of smell will fade away, but my data of my location, my movement, my stay time in a place, my speed, my steps, my beat of heart will all be recorded and exposed as a chart of data shown on the interface. These data can be used and seen by others. But I don't want others to see my trace and know my personal attributes, what should I do? To start this study and figure out what my trace of footprint would look like, I start an experiment in the campus.

My steps, my movement location, heart beat and time of activities are also recorded.

My idea after this experiment

I would say the output of my activities are so sensitive that cannot be shown by others. So what should I do to hide my data to others but only I know my activities through out the day? I think about use a camouflage method to translate my bare data into a form of secret visualization.

This visualization can be a 2D auto generated image that people can personalize their own shapes of indicators. It can also be a 3D xyz axis modeling that people can see it as a wave or a conceptual shape.

Reflection

I'm always worried about my data leaking when the digital platforms record my data. I can't change the system of the algorithm or the policy of the digital detector but the camouflage is a physical hide that have both security and beauty for us.

I think it is safe to share the outcome visualization to the internet because no one can guess or try to analyze my own attributes through my activities data trace.
